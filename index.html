DISTANCE

As the name suggests, the distance is going to be more than a marathon distance. This year, it is going to be 50 KM

VENUE

The race will start and end at Decathlon, Sarjapur Road. Total elevation gain for the course is 450 mts

REGISTRATION

The registration fee is Rs. 1500/- and entitles your entry for the supported run. A unique tee and a medal as a souvenir. 

AID STATIONS

There will be total 8 aid stations with electrolytes, fresh fruits, cookies, chikki etc. Aid Stations are placed after every 7 km through out the course. 

CREW AND MARSHALS

Route marshals will be available on motor bikes, and bicycles throughout the course to guide and help the runners. 

MEDICAL

Aid stations will be equipped with basic medical kits such as pain relief spray, first aid kit

Frequenty Asked Questions

Is there a prize money ?

As the name suggests, this is one of a unique run, where the focus is the experience the joy of running. Hence, there is no offcial prize money yet.

How about hydration support ?

Throughout the race course, we will be providing electrolytes, fruits, buiscuits, sweets etc.
